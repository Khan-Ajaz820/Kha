What is EMOJI MOSAIC?

Emoji Mosaic is a modern type of digital art where your images are transformed or maked using very tiny emojis. This creates a unique and visually appealing image. In an emoji mosaic, every pixel of the photo is replaced by a tiny emoji that matches the color of that part of the image. When all these emojis are combined, they blend together and form a beautiful final picture.
This form of art is becoming very popular nowadays on social media because of its unique look. When you zoom out, the emoji mosaic looks like your original image, but when you zoom in, you can clearly see each individual emoji tile.
